Fun Facts About Dogs

Did you know that dogs have been our loyal companions for over 15,000 years? They have a powerful sense of smell, which is estimated to be between 10,000 to 100,000 times more acute than that of humans. The Basenji, a breed from Central Africa, is known as the "barkless dog" due to its unique yodel-like sound instead of a traditional bark. Dogs also have their own unique nose print, just like human fingerprints!
